Blood Bank Management System (BBMS) is an online portal. BBMS is basically a system that helps donors/requestor to register themselves, check blood availability,search for donor,request blood as well as check the approval status of requested blood, donate blood and check status of blood donated. The administrator of blood bank can approve donation of blood as well as approve blood to requestor. There are 5 main functonalities in this application. The user that is donor or requester need to register in blood bank portal. The registered users can check blood availability and can place a request if the required blood group is available. The donor can make a donation and can check the status of donated blood.The admin can approve/reject request of both requestors and donors based on requirements and availability.
